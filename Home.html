Our Team

Education

Show Central, alongside hosting many attractions, is also an educational server which aims to educate guests about the animals we have on the server. We do this by hosting animal feeding shows in which a cast member tells the audience about the animal while feeding them, information boards which tell guests essential facts about the animal and by operating as a realistic zoo where the staff members actually go into the enclosures and make sure the animals are okay- the public is able to watch this. We are also proud to be a family-friendly server and uphold rules, such as no swearing, to give parents peace of mind and to keep the server a friendly place to be. Finally, we also give parts of our donations to wildlife trusts in the UK in order to help real life animals out as well as our virtual animals.
